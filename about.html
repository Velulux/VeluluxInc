About Velulux

Welcome to Velulux, where timeless elegance meets precision engineering. Founded with a passion for horology and a commitment to quality, Velulux has rapidly become a trusted name in the world of luxury timepieces.

Our Mission
At Velulux, our mission is to craft watches that are not only stylish and sophisticated but also reliable and affordable. We believe that a great watch is a perfect blend of form and function, a companion for every moment of your life.

Design Philosophy
Designed in Switzerland, the heartland of watchmaking, our timepieces embody the heritage and craftsmanship that Switzerland is renowned for. Each watch is meticulously assembled in Hong Kong, ensuring every piece meets our high standards of precision and durability.

Quality and Craftsmanship
We take pride in using premium materials and state-of-the-art manufacturing techniques. From our stainless steel cases to our hardened mineral glass crystals, every component is chosen for its superior quality and longevity.

Customer Trust
With over 200,000 satisfied customers in Singapore and Qatar, Velulux has built a reputation for excellence and trust. Our commitment to customer satisfaction is reflected in the positive feedback and loyalty of our clientele.
